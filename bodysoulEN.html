The focus of the BodySoul Spa project is the homepage, here I used a beautiful background image that shows a cherry tree and fills the entire screen. After that I used CSS3 (@keyframes) to make the falling petals.

The button on the home page leads to a very simple second page, which has a strip of the previous image as a header and footer, in addition to a slider containing images of the Spa and a small paragraph ending in a call to action.
